Milk and cereal

Description

You'll learn how to make a totally normal and conventional bowl of milk and cereal.

Ingredients:

  • milk
  • cereal
  • bowl
  • teaspoon
  • tablespoon (if you are fat)

Preparation:

  1. Put some milk in the bowl.
  2. Put some cereal in the bowl.
  3. Use the spoon to eat.
