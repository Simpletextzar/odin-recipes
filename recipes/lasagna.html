Lasagna Recipe

Description

You'll discover how to make the most delicious lasagna ever.

Ingredients:

  • lasagna pasta
  • tomatoes
  • minced meat
  • cheese
  • garlic
  • butter
  • Super Instant Lasagna

Preparation:

  1. Unpack all of your ingredients.
  2. Put everything inside some bowls.
  3. Now unpack the Super Instant Lasagna.
  4. Put the Super Instant Lasagna in the microwave for 5 minutes.
  5. Get the Super Instant Lasagna out of the microwave.
  6. Eat the Super Instant Lasagna.
